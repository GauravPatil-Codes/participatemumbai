About the Platform

Participate Mumbai is BMC's single-window platform that streamlines public participation, corporate CSR initiatives, and donations to strengthen urban resilience. Launched in May 2025, this innovative platform brings together citizens, corporates, and government officials to create a more engaged and sustainable Mumbai.

Streamline Public Participation

Institutionalize public participation at AC level with structured engagement processes.

Foster Collaboration

Build bridges between BMC officials, citizens, and corporates for effective partnerships.

Ensure Transparency

Maintain accountability through structured engagement and transparent processes.
